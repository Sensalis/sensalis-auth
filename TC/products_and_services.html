3  Products and Services

3.1 Description of your products and services

The MD900 was produced between 1995 and 1997. It is expected that the existing fleet still stands at 85 units.

The MD902 Explorer was produced between 1997 and 2022. It is expected that the existing fleet still stands at approximately 135 units.

Fleet holders would profit from a new supply of spare parts and upgrades.

Hence, revenues can be generated from:

  • Spare parts sales for the existing fleet.
  • Maintenance and upgrade services.
  • New helicopter sales.
