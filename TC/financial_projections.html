4.1 Inflation

The following cash flow model estimates the annual price inflation for costs and personnel to be 1,5%.

The price inflation for helicopters sold, however, is at 3.5% for the first 3 years, and is reduced to 2% after the market entry of marenco-aviation GmbH, because of the changing market dynamics.

4.5 Company Valuation

4.5.1 Corporate Tax

As of 1st January 2024 Switzerland introduced the OECD minimum taxation for international companies of 15% for consolidated revenues of the group of more than EUR 750 Million per year.1 As EUR differs from the base currency of the calculations CHF, the accumulated turnover over any 12 months will be converted into Swiss Francs at the current EURCHF rate. At this point of the model, we do not add any fluctuations in the foreign exchange rate for this purpose.

There is a great likelihood that the hurdle annual consolidated revenues may be reached, and the actual corporate tax rate of 12.6% (see facts and figures on the website of Kontaktstelle Wirtschaft, Kanton Glarus) will be increased to 15% towards the end of our cash flow model.

We assume that taxes are paid in the same month as they occur. There is no treatment of pre- or post-payment of taxes, nor any interest earned or paid taxes being paid earlier or later.

4.5.2 Company Value over Time

For the visualisation of the company value over thime we assumed:
